"AI NPC" challenge
Format · 18K videos · Gaming
Rising
Concept
Let AI fully control your in-game decisions for a full match — no overrides. Film your live reaction and add commentary on what the AI got right and catastrophically wrong.
Hook
"I gave AI full control of my game for 60 seconds. It found a strategy I'd never try — and it almost worked."
Intro (0–5s)
Cut straight to gameplay. No intro. First line: your hook. Let the AI's first bad decision happen before you say anything.
Build (5–35s)
Commentary over 3 key AI decisions — one funny fail, one surprisingly smart, one unhinged. Keep energy high.
Payoff (35–55s)
The final outcome. Your genuine reaction is the content. Freeze the best moment. End on a question for comments.
Outro (55–60s)
CTA: "Should I let AI control my ranked matches all week?" — drives follow for the series.
Alternative hooks
"What if the worst player in your lobby was actually AI? I tested it."
"I let a bot play ranked for me. It went better than expected. Then much worse."
